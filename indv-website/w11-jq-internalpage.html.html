Interviewing

We want you to succeed in building your gift so we have provided resources that can help you along the way. In order to begin the creation of your Gift to Remember you need to collect the content. One way to do this is through interviewing your loved one or those who were close to them. Many feel overwhelmed at this process. It may seem daunting, but these links to interview questions and tips will help you make the most of your interviews.

Do you have relatives that live far away? Send them a link to our website that will allow them to record their stories directly into our program. We provide a variety of options for you to choose to fit your preference and budget.
