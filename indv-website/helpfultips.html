Helpful Tips

One of the biggest tips we can give you is to take these projcts one step at a time. Compiling stories aboout one's life can seem overwhelming. To make this process seem more feasable, here is a list of steps that can help you:

  1. Think about the WHY behind the project. This will help you stay motivated
  2. Grab a notebook and start sketching your outline with points you definitely want in your project. (Ex: Important dates, photos and favorite experiences in your loved one's life.)
  3. Write down a list of interview questions for your loved one or for others close to them
  4. Set up times for your interviews
  5. Record Interviews
  6. Start filling out your outline with information you have gathered
  7. Start your search for photos, videos and audio recordings
  8. Upload media content to our program
  9. Send your book to one of our hired editors for review(not required)
  10. Design using our online toolds
  11. Place order
